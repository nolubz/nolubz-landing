FAQ

ArqaSave questions, answered.

Can I withdraw my savings early?▾
Yes. You can withdraw from Personal and Calendar Savings at any time. An early withdrawal fee applies to Calendar Savings if you exit before your target date. For Group Savings, early withdrawal rules depend on the group cycle stage — the group admin sets these terms.
How does Group Savings prevent fraud?▾
All members are KYC-verified Nolubz users. Contributions are locked and tracked automatically — no one can skip a payment or take someone else's payout. The cycle order is set at the start and cannot be changed without group consensus.
Can I have multiple savings goals?▾
Yes. You can run multiple Personal goals, Calendar plans, and Group Savings simultaneously — all managed from your ArqaSave dashboard.
How many people can join a Group Savings circle?▾
Group Savings circles support between 2 and 20 members. Each member must have a verified Nolubz account.
What currency are ArqaSave balances held in?▾
All ArqaSave balances are held in NOLZ — 1 NOLZ = 1 USD, always. Your savings never lose value due to exchange rate changes while held in ArqaSave.
