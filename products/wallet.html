FAQ

Wallet questions, answered.

Is there a fee to deposit money?▾
No. Deposits into your NOLZ Wallet are always completely free — 0% deposit fee, no minimum, no hidden charges. You can add funds via bank transfer, debit/credit card, or crypto.
What does 1 NOLZ = 1 USD really mean?▾
It means your balance never fluctuates inside the platform. 1 NOLZ is permanently pegged to 1 USDT which equals 1 USD. Currency conversion only happens at the deposit or withdrawal point — your internal balance is always stable.
How do I send money from my wallet?▾
Tap Send in your dashboard, enter the recipient's wallet address or username, enter the amount in NOLZ, confirm with your PIN, and the transfer is done instantly.
Is there a withdrawal fee?▾
Yes. Withdrawals carry a small fee starting at 0.5%, which decreases as your monthly transaction volume increases. The fee is always shown clearly before you confirm — no surprises.
How do I receive money?▾
Share your unique NOLZ wallet address or QR code with the sender. Once they send, the funds appear in your wallet instantly with a push notification.
