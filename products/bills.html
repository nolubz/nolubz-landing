FAQ

Bills questions, answered.

How quickly do bill payments go through?▾
Bill payments and airtime top-ups are processed instantly — typically within seconds of confirmation. You receive a receipt immediately and the recipient's service is topped up right away.
How does cashback work?▾
When providers pay a commission to Nolubz for processing your payment, we split 60% of that commission back to you as cashback — credited directly to your NOLZ wallet within seconds. No redemption needed.
Can I pay bills for someone in a different country?▾
Yes. You can pay bills and top up airtime or data for anyone, anywhere in the world — regardless of where you are. Just enter their meter number, phone number, or account details and confirm.
What networks are supported for airtime?▾
Nolubz supports 150+ mobile networks across 130+ countries via the Reloadly global network — covering virtually every major carrier worldwide.
Is there a fee for paying bills?▾
You pay the face value of the bill or top-up — no hidden charges. The cashback you receive more than covers any processing costs, making Nolubz one of the most cost-effective ways to pay bills internationally.
