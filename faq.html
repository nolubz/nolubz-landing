🌍 General
What is Nolubz?▾
Nolubz is a global financial platform that lets you send money internationally, save toward goals with ArqaSave, protect transactions with Xecro escrow, and pay bills in 40+ countries worldwide — all from one beautifully designed app.
Who is Nolubz for?▾
Nolubz is built for anyone who moves, lives, works, or transacts across borders. Whether you're a student paying tuition internationally, a freelancer receiving payment from a global client, someone supporting family abroad, or a business paying suppliers — Nolubz is designed for you.
Which countries does Nolubz support?▾
Nolubz currently supports 40+ countries across Africa, Europe, the Americas, the Middle East, Asia, and the Pacific — including the UK, USA, Canada, France, Germany, UAE, India, Philippines, Brazil, and many more. We expand to new markets every quarter.
What languages does Nolubz support?▾
Nolubz supports 9 languages: English, French, Arabic, Spanish, Portuguese, Hindi, Indonesian, Vietnamese, and Tagalog. Your language is set automatically when you select your country during registration.
💳 NOLZ Wallet
What is NOLZ and why is it always $1?▾
NOLZ is the Nolubz currency unit. 1 NOLZ is permanently pegged to 1 USDT which equals 1 USD. This means your wallet balance never fluctuates due to exchange rate changes inside the platform. When you deposit or withdraw in local currency, conversion happens at that exact moment — giving you a permanently stable internal balance.
Are deposits really free?▾
Yes. Deposits into your NOLZ Wallet are always completely free — 0% deposit fee, no minimum amount, no hidden charges, anywhere in the world. You can add funds via bank transfer, debit/credit card, or crypto.
Is there a fee to withdraw?▾
Yes. Withdrawals carry a small fee starting at 0.5%, which decreases as your monthly transaction volume increases. The fee is always shown clearly before you confirm — no surprises.
How do I send money from my wallet?▾
Tap Send in your dashboard, enter the recipient's Nolubz wallet address or username, enter the amount, confirm with your PIN, and the transfer is completed instantly.
How do I receive money?▾
Share your unique NOLZ wallet address or QR code with the sender. Funds appear in your wallet instantly with a push notification confirming the receipt.
🔐 Xecro Smart Escrow
What is Xecro?▾
Xecro is Nolubz's smart escrow service. It holds funds securely in a vault until both parties are satisfied with a deal — protecting buyers from fraud and guaranteeing payment for sellers. It works for any transaction: freelance work, buying goods online, business deals, and more.
What is the difference between Xecro Basic and Xecro Pro?▾
Xecro Basic is for straightforward deals — one payment, one delivery, one release. Xecro Pro is for complex, milestone-based deals where payment is split into stages. Each milestone is funded and released independently as work is completed and approved.
What happens if there's a dispute?▾
Either party can raise a dispute. The Nolubz Xecro team reviews all submitted evidence — deal terms, delivery proof, communication — and mediates a fair resolution. Funds remain in the vault throughout the dispute process.
Can Xecro be used for cross-border deals?▾
Yes. Xecro works across all 40+ countries supported by Nolubz. The same protection applies regardless of where the buyer and seller are located.
🏦 ArqaSave
What is ArqaSave?▾
ArqaSave is Nolubz's savings feature with three modes: Personal Savings (save toward a goal at your own pace), Calendar Savings (automatic daily, weekly, or monthly contributions toward a target date), and Group Savings (rotating circles where members contribute together and each person receives the pot in turn).
Can I withdraw savings early?▾
Yes. Personal Savings can be withdrawn at any time with a small early withdrawal fee. Calendar Savings carry a fee if you exit before the target date. Group Savings early exit depends on the cycle stage and group rules set at creation.
How many people can join a Group Savings circle?▾
Group Savings circles support between 2 and 20 members. Every member must have a verified Nolubz account.
Are ArqaSave balances stable?▾
Yes. All ArqaSave balances are held in NOLZ — 1 NOLZ = 1 USD, always. Your savings never lose value due to exchange rate changes while held in ArqaSave.
⚡ Bills & Airtime
Can I pay bills for someone in another country?▾
Yes. You can pay bills and top up airtime or data for anyone, anywhere in the world — regardless of your own location. Enter their meter number, phone number, or account details and confirm.
How does cashback work?▾
When providers pay a commission to Nolubz for processing your payment, we split 60% of that commission back to you as real cashback — credited directly to your NOLZ wallet within seconds. Not points, not vouchers — actual NOLZ you can send or spend immediately.
Which networks are supported for airtime?▾
Nolubz supports 150+ mobile networks across 130+ countries via the Reloadly global network — covering virtually every major carrier worldwide.
🔒 Security
How secure is Nolubz?▾
Nolubz uses end-to-end encryption (AES-256), multi-tier KYC identity verification, transaction PIN protection, real-time fraud monitoring, and works exclusively with licensed, regulated payment partners — Stripe, Crossmint, Monnify, Paystack, and TransFi.
Is my personal data ever sold?▾
Never. Nolubz will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used only to operate your account and improve your experience.
What happens if I see a transaction I don't recognize?▾
Contact us immediately at support@nolubz.com. Our security team reviews all disputed transactions urgently and can place a hold on your account while we investigate.
