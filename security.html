Security pillars

Six layers of protection.

Every transaction on Nolubz passes through multiple independent security checks before it moves a single cent.

🔐

End-to-End Encryption

Every transaction, message, and user record is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 — the same standard used by global financial institutions and governments.

🪪

KYC Identity Verification

Every Nolubz user undergoes multi-tier Know Your Customer (KYC) verification. This ensures only real, verified individuals can access and move funds on the platform.

🔑

Transaction PIN

Every payment, transfer, and withdrawal requires your personal PIN. Nothing leaves your wallet without your explicit, active approval — every single time.

🔔

Real-Time Alerts

Every movement in your wallet triggers an instant push notification. You are always the first to know about any activity — and anything unexpected can be reported immediately.

📊

Transparent Ledger

Every debit and credit is recorded with a complete, immutable audit trail. Your transaction history is always accurate, complete, and available to review at any time.

🛡

Fraud Monitoring

Automated systems monitor every transaction for unusual patterns. Suspicious activity triggers immediate holds and user notifications — before damage can occur.
