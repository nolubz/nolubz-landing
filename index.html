FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Everything you need to know about Nolubz — plain and simple.

What is Nolubz?▾
Nolubz is a global financial platform that lets you send money internationally, save toward goals with ArqaSave, protect transactions with Xecro escrow, and pay bills in 40+ countries — all from one app.
What is NOLZ and why is it always $1?▾
NOLZ is the Nolubz currency unit. 1 NOLZ always equals 1 USDT which always equals 1 USD. Your wallet balance never loses value due to exchange rate volatility inside the platform. When you deposit or withdraw in local currency, conversion happens at that exact moment — giving you a permanently stable balance.
Are deposits really free?▾
Yes. Deposits into your Nolubz wallet are always free — 0% deposit fee, no hidden charges, no minimum amount, anywhere in the world. Withdrawals carry a small fee starting at 0.5% which reduces as your monthly transaction volume increases.
What is Xecro and how does it protect me?▾
Xecro is Nolubz's smart escrow service. When you start a deal, the buyer's NOLZ is locked in a secure vault — the seller receives funds only after the buyer confirms delivery. For larger deals, Xecro Pro allows milestone-based payments. Both sides are fully protected from fraud at every step.
Which countries does Nolubz support?▾
Nolubz supports 40+ countries across Africa, Europe, the Americas, the Middle East, Asia, and the Pacific — including the UK, USA, Canada, France, Germany, UAE, Saudi Arabia, India, Philippines, Brazil, and many more. We expand to new markets every quarter.
How does ArqaSave work?▾
ArqaSave has three modes: Personal Savings (save toward a personal goal at your own pace), Calendar Savings (automatic daily, weekly or monthly contributions with a target date), and Group Savings (rotating savings circles with friends and family where each member receives the full pot in turn).
Do I earn cashback on bills and airtime?▾
Yes. When you pay for airtime, data, electricity, cable TV, or education services through Nolubz, a portion of the provider commission is shared back with you as real cashback — credited directly to your wallet. Not points. Real money.
When will Nolubz launch?▾
Nolubz is in final development and launching very soon. Join the waitlist to be among the first to receive access. Waitlist members get priority access and launch updates delivered directly to their inbox.
